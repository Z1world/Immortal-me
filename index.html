THE IDEA

The people we lose leave silences in the moments that matter most.

A daughter turns 30. Her father isn't there. He died three years ago and never got to say what he wanted to say — what he'd been meaning to say.

A wife celebrates her 40th anniversary alone. Her husband knew this day would come. He just never found a way to speak to it.

Immortal Me changes that. Write the letter now. Record the video now. Choose the moment it arrives — their birthday in 2027, their wedding day, their graduation — and we'll make sure it finds them.

Your words, delivered exactly when they're needed most.
